About Me

I am a Senior Data / AI / Cloud Engineer currently working at GARAIO AG, placed as a Data Engineer at SBB (Swiss Federal Railways) in Bern, Switzerland. I bring over 20 years of experience across a diverse range of languages, platforms, and roles — from Java and C# through to Python, and from desktop GUIs to large-scale distributed SOA systems.

My current focus is on data engineering, AI, and cloud. I hold a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bath, and have completed a CAS in Advanced Machine Learning and a CAS in NLP at the University of Bern. I have earned multiple Microsoft Azure certifications in AI and data analytics. I am an active Kaggle participant and am actively building NLP / transformer prototypes and integrating OpenAI APIs into real-world projects.

My analytical foundation comes from a Theoretical and Mathematical Physics degree from the University of Edinburgh. Throughout my career I have progressed from developer to architect, technical lead, technical manager, CTO, and mentor — including within the Berlin start-up scene at companies like Next Big Thing AG and as co-founder of Infiindia.

I believe strongly in design patterns, unit testing, clean code, and sound methodology, regardless of the technology stack. I am fluent in English and German.
